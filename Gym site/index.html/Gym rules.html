Gym Rules:

The following rules should be strictly followed to ensure social distancing and real time monitoring of gym usage.
1. No loud music should be played at any time, any such reports the music system will be put out of action.
2. Only a maximum of 10 persons are allowed inside the gym including the trainers at a particular time.
3. Maintain 6ft. distance with the fellow gym-user at all times, and use mask when not exercising (rest between sets).
4. Wearing sports shoes and carrying a towel is mandatory.
5. All the instruments/weight blocks must be put back into their place after use.
6. Use of ground or open space outside the gym for free hand exercise is promoted so as to maximize use of gym facilities.
7. All users should limit their time spend in gym to max 75mins in a session to enable more members to use the facility.
SANITIZE, WASH HAND, WEAR MASK, MAINTAIN SOCIAL DISTANCING.
